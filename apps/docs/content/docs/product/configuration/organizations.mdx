title: Organizations
description: Manage organizations, projects, and team access.
---

Organizations group projects and team members. Each project has its own API keys, traces, and monitor configuration.

Create organizations and projects from the sidebar. Invite team members from organization settings.

## Self-hosted

The first signup becomes the **global admin** with access to everything. After that, new signups require an invitation unless `ALLOW_OPEN_SIGNUP=true` is set. Control organization creation with `ALLOW_ORG_CREATION`.
