Observations monitor your traces in the background and surface issues as findings - ranked by impact with a description and suggested fix.

![Observations tab showing AI-generated findings ranked by impact with descriptions and suggested fixes](/docs/observations.webp)

## Findings

Each finding is ranked by impact - **high**, **medium**, or **low** - and includes a description of what went wrong and a suggestion for how to fix it. The AI looks across traces to identify patterns, not just one-off issues.

Dismiss findings you've handled. The AI deduplicates automatically so you won't see the same issue twice.

## Setup

1. Go to your project settings and create an observation
2. Choose which trace names to watch (or leave empty for all)
3. Set a sampling rate (1-100%) to control how many traces get evaluated
4. Optionally add custom heuristics to guide what the AI looks for

## Requirements

Requires an [AI provider](/docs/product/settings/ai-provider) to be configured in your project settings.
