---
title: Scan & Investigation
description: How the two monitoring agents work together.
---

## Scan agent

Runs periodically (default every 5 minutes) when new traces arrive. It queries recent traces, compares against what it knows about your project via [memory](/docs/product/monitoring/memory), and creates tickets when it finds concerning patterns.

It looks for patterns, not individual anomalies — hallucination, context loss, intent mismatches, retry loops, cost spikes, instruction drift.

## Investigation agent

Activates when a ticket is created, you leave a comment, or you click investigate manually. It queries your trace data, builds a case, writes a comment with its verdict, and sets priority and labels.

The investigation agent maintains working notes per ticket that persist across runs. If it schedules a follow-up, it picks up where it left off.

## Cost controls

Both agents consume tokens from your AI provider. Set monthly limits and scan intervals in [agent settings](/docs/product/configuration/agent).
