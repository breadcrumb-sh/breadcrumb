title: Overview
description: AI-powered monitoring for your LLM agents.
---

The monitor is a kanban board on your project overview. An AI agent scans your traces and creates tickets when something looks wrong.

{/* TODO: Screenshot of the kanban board */}

Tickets move through four columns: **Queue** → **Investigating** → **Review** → **Done**. You can also create tickets manually for things you want the agent to look into.

Click a ticket to see the agent's findings, leave comments to ask follow-up questions, and manage priority and labels.

The monitor requires an [AI provider](/docs/product/configuration/ai-provider). Configure scan intervals and cost limits in [agent settings](/docs/product/configuration/agent).
