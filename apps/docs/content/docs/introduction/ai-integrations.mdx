## MCP

Connect your editor to Breadcrumb via MCP. Your LLM gets direct access to production traces - the actual prompts sent, responses received, token counts, and costs.

**Debug and improve** - Ask your LLM to look at failing or expensive traces. It reads the real prompts and outputs, identifies the issue, and fixes the code directly - no separate prompt management tool needed.
