---
title: Members
description: Manage team members and roles.
---

Invite members from your organization settings. Roles control what they can access:

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access, can delete organization and projects |
| **Admin** | Manage members, keys, settings |
| **Member** | View and interact with traces and monitor |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access |
